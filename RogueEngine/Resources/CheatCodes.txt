'I' - Teleports to end of stage (All levels except Hub)
'O' - Teleports to start of stage (All levels except Hub)
'0' - Infinite Teleport Ability (Resets on entering new level)
'P' - Enables player to trigger end condition (ONLY IF PLAYER IS IN HUB LEVEL)
'L' - Enables player to trigger true end condition (ONLY IF PLAYER IS IN HUB LEVEL)

Player also slows down time when holding left click
